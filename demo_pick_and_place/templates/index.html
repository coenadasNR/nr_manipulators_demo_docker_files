Experience the Xarm 5: Live Demonstrations with Digital Twins

Discover the impressive capabilities of the Xarm 5 robotic arm as we showcase its versatility through a series of engaging demonstrations. We further explore how NVIDIA Isaac Sim is utilized to create high-fidelity digital twins of the Xarm 5 and its environment, enabling comprehensive simulation, testing, and optimization of robotic workflows in a virtual space before deployment in the real world.

Demo 1: Vision-based pick and place task.

Watch a smart vision-based pick-and-place system in action. Each cube is identified by a unique ArUco marker, allowing the system to accurately detect and sort them. Cubes are seamlessly moved from their pickup point to the correct destination based on the information encoded in their respective ArUco markers.

Simulated + Real Demo

Please verify the below steps to run the demo.

Switched on Xarm5

Check the Xarm5 is turned on. This is shown by the green light on the controller box.

Emergency Stop Button

Emergency Stop button must be at a close distance to the user so it can be pressed easily during emergency.

Place cubes in camera view

Ensure the cube are within the camera views on the table.

Remove any obtsacles

No obstacles should be near the workspace of the robot.

Safe Distance

Please maintain a safe distance from the robot while the demo is running.
